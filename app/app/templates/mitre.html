How to read these heat maps

Observed Coverage

Direct evidence from PCAP traffic and normalized forwarded logs. This answers: what was actually seen?

Theoretical Coverage

Techniques supported by detected log sources only, mapped through the selected ATT&CK STIX bundle's data-source/data-component metadata. OS and product assumptions do not increase theoretical coverage.

Validated Coverage

Detection Rules or Network Rules that matched evidence. This answers: what detection content fired?

All metrics use the same applicable ATT&CK denominator. Techniques outside the detected OS/log-source scope are gray as Not Applicable and do not lower percentages. A Linux-only or network-only capture is not penalized for missing Windows/macOS-only techniques.
